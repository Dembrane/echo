# dembrane documentation

dembrane helps groups turn what people *say* into something they can *act on*. People
speak - in a workshop, a town hall, a citizen panel, a research interview - and dembrane
records, transcribes securely in dozens of languages, and turns hours of dialogue into
summaries, themes, reports, and a chat you can ask questions of.

We build on one belief: *people know how.* Communities already hold the knowledge to
solve their own challenges. dembrane doesn't add intelligence to a group - it surfaces the
intelligence already there, and keeps the humans in the room firmly in charge.
